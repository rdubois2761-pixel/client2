Our Story

The Planet

Since 1982, The Daily Planet has carved out a hip space for food, local art, and bar banter in the heart of downtown Burlington, Vermont.

Under chef-owner Neil Solis, the menu celebrates Vermont's agricultural community — local chorizo, local steak, local venison, Cabot cheddar, and fresh produce from the Intervale Community Farm.

Creative, globally inspired cuisine rooted in New England. Whether you're here for drinks, a quick bite, or a long dinner with friends — there's a corner of the Planet for everyone.
